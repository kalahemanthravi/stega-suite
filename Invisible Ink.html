How it Works

Invisible Ink uses Zero-Width Steganography. It converts your encrypted secret message into a sequence of zero-width characters (invisible to the eye) and embeds them inside normal text.

  • Hide: We take your secret, encrypt it with your password, turn it into invisible binary markers (U+200B & U+200C), and inject it into your Cover Text.
  • Reveal: We extract the invisible markers, decode them back to binary, decrypt them with your password, and reveal the secret.
