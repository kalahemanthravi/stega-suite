2. "Burn After Reading" Mechanics

                    
The "Burn After Reading" feature utilizes Client-Side Local Storage. When a
                        message is viewed, it is marked as 'read' on that specific browser and device. We do not
                        maintain a central database to track views globally. Therefore, while the link will not open
                        again on the same device, it may theoretically be accessible on a different device
                        until the time expiration is reached. This is a privacy feature, not a Digital Rights Management
                        (DRM) guarantee.

                


                

                    
3. Privacy & No-Log Policy

                    
This tool operates entirely in your browser (Client-Side). We do not have servers that store your
                        messages, passwords, or encryption keys. We cannot view, recover, or delete your messages
                        because we do not possess them. The encrypted data resides solely within the URL generated.
