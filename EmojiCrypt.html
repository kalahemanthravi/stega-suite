Tool Guide

What is this? Emoji Crypt is a fun yet secure tool that turns your text into a sequence of emojis. It uses military-grade encryption, so the emojis are not just random—they hold your actual message!

How to use:

  • Encrypt: Type your message, set a password, and get a string of emojis. Send these emojis to a friend.
  • Decrypt: Switch to the Decrypt tab, paste the emoji string you received, enter the password, and read the hidden message.
