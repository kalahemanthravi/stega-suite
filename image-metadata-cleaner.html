How It Works

What is Metadata? Digital photos often contain hidden information called "Exif data" (camera settings, capture date, and sometimes precise GPS location).

Using this tool:

  • The tool immediately scans and identifies hidden data tags.
  • It automatically creates a "Clean" copy of the image with all metadata stripped out.
