2. Privacy Policy

We prioritize your privacy through our architecture:

  • Zero Knowledge: We do not have access to your files, passwords, or messages.
  • Local Processing: All encryption, decryption, and audio processing occur entirely within your browser using the Web Audio and Web Crypto APIs. No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or trackers to monitor your activity within this application.

3. Technical Security

We employ military-grade encryption standards: AES-256-GCM for data encryption and PBKDF2-SHA256 (100,000 iterations) for key derivation. While robust, no system is completely immune to all forms of attack (e.g., coercion, keyloggers on your device).
